Turtles

Crisol

Turts are cool. I have visited Akumal, Mexico just to swim with turtles. The city of Akumal is known for their turtles, and the word akumal actually translates to city of the turtles. But here are some cool turtle facts:

  1. Turtles have an "egg tooth" that they use to break open their shells when they hatch.
  2. The gender of sea turtles is determined by the temperature of the nest.
  3. If the nest is warmer more females will be born, if it is colder more males will be born.
  4. A group of sea turtles is called a bale.
